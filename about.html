About Me

My name is Awisha Pandey and I am a cheerful Class 9 student from Daffodil School. So far I have had a very rewarding and inspiring education as I do my best to perform well in the course work and grab every new opportunity presented. Even aside from school, I am actively involved in various extracurricular practices which include my efforts and motivation. I love playing basketball, a game that I find challenging in terms of team coordination and accompanied by strategic practices. Dance and music forms a great part of my life as well. Often I sing whether it is alone or with friends which is stimulating and effective and when it comes to dancing I feel alive when performing and moving around.

Engaging in these particular interests along with my studies helps me remain optimistic and motivated. I look forward, to every given possibility, I will rise to pursue my passions while still carrying on my education. This is why each and every one of us aims for progress every day, and it is why I am here, chasing my goals but enjoying the ride.
